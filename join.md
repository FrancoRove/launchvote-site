# Join a LaunchVote Session

You've been invited to a LaunchVote session.

## If you have LaunchVote installed

Tap the invite link directly from a message — the app will open automatically and take you straight to the session.

If the app doesn't open, launch LaunchVote and tap **Join Session** from the home screen, then enter the join code from your invitation.

## If you don't have LaunchVote yet

LaunchVote will be available on the App Store soon. Once installed, tap **Join Session** from the home screen and enter the join code from your invitation.
