# LaunchVote Support

Welcome to LaunchVote support. We're here to help you get the most out of LaunchVote — the app that helps groups decide where to go together.

## Contact Support

If you need help, reach out to us:

**Email:** launchvoteapp@proton.me

We do our best to respond promptly.

## What We Can Help With

- **Login problems** — trouble signing in with email, Google, Apple, or as a guest.
- **Username setup** — questions about creating or finding your username.
- **Friends** — sending or accepting friend requests, viewing your friends list.
- **Friend groups** — creating, editing, or deleting friend groups/crews.
- **Session invites** — sending invites, inviting a group, or receiving targeted invites.
- **Joining sessions** — issues with join codes or invite links.
- **Voting and session issues** — trouble suggesting places, voting, or viewing results.
- **Account deletion** — questions about deleting your account or data.
- **Privacy questions** — questions about your data or our privacy policy.
- **Bug reports** — something not working as expected.

## Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly in the app:

1. Open **LaunchVote**.
2. Tap **Account** at the bottom of the home screen.
3. Tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm when prompted.

Your account and associated data will be permanently removed.

### If Deletion Fails

If you see a message asking you to sign out and sign back in:

1. Tap **Sign Out** in the Account screen.
2. Sign back in with the same method you used before (email, Google, or Apple).
3. Go back to **Account** and try **Delete Account** again.

If you are still unable to delete your account, contact us at the email above and we will assist you.

## Troubleshooting

If something isn't working as expected, try these steps:

1. **Update the app** — make sure you have the latest version from the App Store.
2. **Check your internet connection** — LaunchVote requires an internet connection to load sessions, suggestions, and votes.
3. **Sign out and sign back in** — this can resolve stale session or authentication issues.
4. **Confirm your join code** — join codes are 6 characters, uppercase letters and numbers. Make sure there are no extra spaces.
5. **Allow location access** — if you want to search for nearby places, make sure location permission is enabled in your device's Settings.
6. **Restart the app** — close LaunchVote completely and reopen it.

If the issue persists after trying these steps, contact support with a description of what happened.
